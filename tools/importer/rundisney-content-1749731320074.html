Celebrate the Incredible Journeys of Disney Princesses with the 2026 Disney Princess Half Marathon Weekend!

Stay Connected

  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • Youtube
Share
by runDisney
June 03, 2025

Each and every Disney Princess has embarked on her own extraordinary journey. During the 2026 Disney Princess Half Marathon Weekend, runDisney athletes can celebrate those journeys – and embark on their very own – over five magic-filled days at Walt Disney World Resort. No matter which race (or races) you choose, you’ll be immersed in everything from meet and greets with rarely seen Characters and a sensational soundtrack to inspiring movie clips and more. And once the running is done, wrap up your royal weekend with some Disney Princess Yoga at Disney’s Hollywood Studios.

Today we are sharing a first look at the individual event themes:

Disney Princess 5K | Merida
Get ready to channel your inner Merida on this 3.1 mile journey where you can embrace your fate—or change it.

Disney Princess 10K | Moana
Set sail and wayfind your way through 6.2 miles of this race hosted by Moana!

Disney Princess Half Marathon | Rapunzel
Let your hair down and join us for magical 13.1 miles hosted by Rapunzel!

Disney Fairytale Challenge | Belle
Be our Guest for 19.3 miles of this challenge (10K & Half Marathon) inspired by everyone’s favorite beauty, Belle, and earn three treasured medals!

Disney Princess Yoga
Breathe in, breathe out and end your royal race weekend with an early morning stretch in Disney's Hollywood Studios during Disney Princess Yoga.

Runners wanting to join for the 2026 event should mark their calendars for the following registration dates and times:

Disney Princess Half Marathon Weekend at Walt Disney World Resort

  • Club runDisney Gold & Platinum Member Registration | June 17, 2025
  • General Registration | June 24, 2025 at 10 AM ET

Get ready to celebrate the journeys some of our beloved Disney Princesses went on to get to where they are—and embark on one of your own—with the 2026 Disney Princess Half Marathon Weekend. Let us know which theme you’re most excited about in social using #PrincessHalf!
